Currants Spiced Jam

This newest addition to the local jam family will add an unforgettable experience paired with many semi-sweet or sweet breakfast dishes or desserts that nod to the popular currant roll that inspired this creation.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Weigh a 1/2 lb of dry currants and place into a blender with 4-5 cups of water and blend into a puree.
  2. Transfer puree into a container and mix in ground cinnamon, clove, ground ginger, pectin, sugar until uniformly mixed.
  3. Transfer the puree into a saucepan over high heat.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil and stir constantly to prevent scorching.
  5. As the jam thickens and reaches desired consistency, remove from the heat.
  6. If using refractometer, a reading of 65° Brix indicates that the jam is ready.
